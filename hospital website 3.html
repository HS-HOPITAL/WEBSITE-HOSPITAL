About H.S. Hospital

H.S. Hospital is a leading healthcare institution located at Gagan Tiraha, Chandausi Road in Moradabad, committed to providing high-quality medical services with a patient-centric approach. Our mission is to deliver compassionate care using modern technology and upholding the highest standards of trust and accessibility for the community.

Founder Message: Dr. Hasan Ali (BUMS), our founder, believes in treating patients like family. His vision is to make healthcare affordable and respectful for all. At H.S. Hospital, we strive to create a welcoming environment where every patient feels safe and cared for.

We offer a full range of healthcare services including general medicine, specialized care, diagnostics, emergency services, and more. Our friendly staff and experienced medical team work tirelessly to ensure each patient receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

Ayushman Bharat Health Card

At H.S. Hospital, we welcome Ayushman Card holders. Eligible patients can avail free and cashless treatment under the Ayushman Bharat scheme. Present your Ayushman Health Card at the reception to receive the following benefits:

  • Up to ₹5,00,000 coverage per family per year
  • No paperwork – cashless and seamless billing
  • All major treatments and surgeries covered
  • No cap on family size or age

Ayushman Card ensures quality healthcare is accessible to those who need it most.

OPD and IPD Services

Outpatient Department (OPD)

Timings: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM (All Days)

  • Contact Reception or WhatsApp to book your slot.
  • Arrive at scheduled time for consultation.
  • Complete prescription and lab tests as advised.
  • Pay at billing counter (cashless for Ayushman Card holders).

Inpatient Department (IPD)

Availability: 24/7 emergency and admission services.

  • Emergency admissions available round the clock.
  • Bring identity & address proof for registration.
  • Private and general ward accommodation.
  • Ayushman Card patients get cashless hospitalization.
